Bridging the Gap Between
Life and Hope

A centralized ecosystem connecting verified donors , NGOs, Hospitals , Blood Banks and Recipients.
We are here with digital solution platform "Lifynk" improving the manual coordination in blood donation process by providing a seamless interface for all stakeholders.

Donor Registration

Securely sign up and provide documentation for NGO verification, building a trusted verified donor pool.

NGO Approved

Validation of every donor request and camp coordination to eliminate fake information across the network.

Hospital Verification

Access real-time blood stock across nearby blood banks and trigger emergency broadcasts when supply is critically low.

Blood Bank

Conduct full medical screening, store donor reports as a digital Medical Resume, and manage real-time blood stock inventory.

Recipient

Instantly locate compatible verified blood units nearby and receive emergency donor notifications during the Golden Hour.
