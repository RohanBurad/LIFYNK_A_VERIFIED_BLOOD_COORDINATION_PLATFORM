Technical Workflow

How Lifynk Bridges
the Gaps

Our platform utilizes a structured 5-tier coordination system—integrating Verified Entities, Location-Based Logic, and Real-Time Management to ensure every drop counts.

01

Donor Registration & Verification

Every donor registers on the platform and submits their medical documentation. NGOs thoroughly verify eligibility through a strict KYC process, ensuring only genuine and medically fit donors enter the verified donor pool.

02

NGO Approval & Medical Screening

Blood Banks conduct full medical screening on verified donors. Reports are stored digitally as a Medical Resume, building a trusted and traceable record for every donor across the network.

03

Smart GPS Matching Engine

Donors are guided to nearby NGOs, Blood Banks, and Donation Camps through a live GPS-powered map. The platform instantly scans for the nearest compatible blood group when a request is raised.

04

Hospital Emergency Broadcast

When blood stock runs critically low, Hospitals trigger an emergency broadcast that instantly notifies the nearest pre-verified compatible donors, reducing response time during the Golden Hour.

05

Real-Time Blood Stock Management

Blood Banks maintain and update live inventory across the network. Hospitals can access real-time blood stock from nearby banks, ensuring complete transparency and zero coordination gaps.
