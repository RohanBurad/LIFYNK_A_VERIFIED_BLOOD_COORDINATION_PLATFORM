Smart HealthCare Platform

Connecting the Blood Donation Community at a Single Platform - Lifynk

Lifynk eliminates fake requests, reduces critical delays, and connects Donors, NGOs, Blood Banks, Hospitals, and Recipients through
a single verified digital ecosystem.

The Challenges Right Now

Coordination Gaps

Manual calls and WhatsApp groups cause unverified, slow, and unreliable blood coordination.

Critical Delays

Absence of real-time data leads to dangerous delays during the Golden Hour.

Verification Risks

Fake donor requests and unverified data put patient lives at risk.

Our Objectives

Strict Verification

Multi-gate verification system eliminating fake requests across the network.

Transparency

Complete audit trails for all blood transactions ensuring full accountability.

Smart Matching

GPS-powered live map connecting donors to nearby NGOs, Blood Banks and Camps.

Platform Features

Live GPS Map

Donors locate nearby NGOs, Blood Banks and Camps in real time.

Medical Resume

Digital donor reports stored securely after full screening.

Verified Donor Pool

Only screened and approved donors enter the active pool.

Emergency Broadcasts

Hospitals notify nearest compatible donors instantly.

Multi-gate Verification

Every request validated before entering the network.

DPDP Act 2023

Fully compliant with India's data protection regulations
